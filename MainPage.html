About Books N' Booze

Welcome to my corner of the world and my little space on the net. Here you will get exposed to my top Books and Booze picks. When I was thinking of what to talk about besides my kids my two favorite things to do do came to mind. Books and Booze! First I thought I would do just booze, but after a few drinks one night I had an epiphany "Books N' Booze!" Well, why not?! So here we find ourselves, me telling you my favorite books along with my favorite booze!

We will first jump into my liquor cabinet and I will share with you what I think is the best in some of the booze catagories. I have my vodka picks for you to gander and encourage you to include your thoughts on my picks and feel free to suggest your own favorites as I am open to trying anything at least once. I even have some bacon flavored vodka along with some Smoked Salmon vodka from Alaska. So please share your experiences!

First Lesson Learned About Booze...

"Just because its high priced and on the top shelf doesn't mean it's the best of the best."

Next we will check out my book case and I will share with you some of my favorite authors and books. As with the booze sections I strongly encourage you to share what you think of the books and or authors as well as share your favorites if its not listed. Make sure that you tell us why you think your pick is a good read and I will most likely pick it up and give it a try.

So to escape my reality I really tend to gravitate towards the sci-fi/fantasy section of the book store. My standard for a good book is a healthy dose of violence, monsters and sex. I am always in the market for new authors and have been pleasantly surprised by some that I would have passed up if it wasn't for someone suggesting them. Because of this I have found some in the romance section that I would never of given a second thought.

First Lesson Learned About Books...

"Don't judge a book by its cover or what section it has been put in!"

About Me!

Well as you already know I am in and from the Pacific Northwest, home of the microbrew beers and where distilled spirits are becoming popular and are very good. I worked in a liquor store for three years and have tried and owned more booze now than I have in my entire life! I can say, it was the one job I had that I LOVED to bring my work home with me.

I haven't outgrown the 'why' phase and went on a quest to find out why and how a lot of the different spirits are made. So here I will share the knowledge that I gleened from various sources and my own personal experiences in trying all the different boozes out there.
My one addiction that is probably the best kind is of books! Before kids I use to read a novel a day, but now three kids, school and a full time job later I am lucky to get three books a week done. My ideal business to own would be a book store that sold new and used books along with a good bottle of booze to enjoy your book to. Of course I would call it Books N' Booze!
